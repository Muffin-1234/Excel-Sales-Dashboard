This project shows regional sales performance, customer trends of various age groups and monthly revenue using Pivot Tables and Interactive Charts.

## Project Highlights
- 3,900 customers generated a total revenue of over £5.18M
- Shows a fully interactive dashboard
- Identifies the top performing product categories and customer segments based on age group.
- Percentage of revenue generated per year is displayed
- Business insights and recommendations were drawn using the visualization

## Key Insights
- Clothing category generated the highest revenue and customer orders
- Male customers contributed the largest share of revenue
- Adults and Seniors represent the strongest purchasing groups
- November recorded the highest customer activity
- 2013 generaed the highest yearly revenue contribution

## Tools & Skills Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Table
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers (Interactive filters)
- Dashboard Design
- Visual & Analytical Storytelling

## Dashboard Features
- Total Revenue by Category
- Gender-based Revenue
- Yearly Revenue Performance
- Payment Method Analysis
- Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
- Interactive Filters
- Insights and Recommendations

  ## Business Recommendations
  - There should be more investment and accessories in clothing and less investment in outerwear.
  - More products should be stocked in the last quarter of the year.
  - There should be discount on feminine products as it will encourage the patronage of the female gender.
